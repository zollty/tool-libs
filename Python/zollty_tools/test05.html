variant

noun
/ˈveəriənt/
/ˈveriənt/, /ˈværiənt/
jump to other results
  1. variant (of/on something) a thing that is a slightly different form or type of something else 变种；变体；变形
    • This game is a variant of baseball. 这种运动是由棒球演变而来的。
    • a variant of the original experiment 原始实验的变体
    • a variant on a familiar theme 熟悉主题的变体

    Word Originlate Middle English (as an adjective in the sense ‘tending to vary’): from Old French, literally ‘varying’, present participle of varier or Latin variare, from varius ‘diverse’ . The noun dates from the mid 19th cent.
