disorderly

adjective
/dɪsˈɔːdəli/
/dɪsˈɔːrdərli/
[usually before noun] (formal)Idioms
jump to other results
  1. ( of people or behaviour 人或行为 ) showing lack of control; publicly noisy or violent 难驾驭的；目无法纪的；骚乱的
    • disorderly behaviour 混乱的行为
    • A disorderly crowd had gathered outside the embassy. 一群不守秩序的人聚集在大使馆外面。
  2. untidy杂乱的；混乱的；凌乱的
    • newspapers in a disorderly pile by the door 门边乱七八糟的一堆报纸
    3. opposite orderly
